Trapped in Time

An Endless Love Story Across Lifetimes
“Can i just love you for the rest of my life?”

He and she seemed to be standing very close at the moment, but in fact, they were 'very far apart.'

To a certain extent, her life was just his 'day.'

There is another issue that sounds a bit unethical.

That is, if he is with her in this life, what would happen in the next? And the one after that? If they continued to stay together in each life, what would become of them?

This question haunted him because his existence was not like hers. His life was an endless cycle, a loop in which he would live, die, and then awaken once more at the age of eighteen, forced to relive the same years over and over. Each time, he would meet her again, and each time, he would fall in love with her all over again.

The reason for considering this issue is precisely because his morality has not yet collapsed, and his concept of time has not completely changed.

And most importantly,

That is, if he is really together with her, then he truly wants to still have this relationship in the next life.

But,

He really had no confidence that he could be with her for 'life after life.'

Even if he could make that choice now, what about in the future? Was he still the same person he was in his first life?

Just say now, how similar is he now to who he was in the first life?

Even, continue to think about it,

You will find that this issue has nothing to do with morality and loyalty to feelings,

But an abyss—if he and she were together for a hundred lifetimes, what kind of state would she be in his eyes?

All her thoughts, actions, and words would become transparent to him... like a complex machine repeating predetermined processes and results.

"This is the problem..."

She recovered from her confusion.

His words were a response to her love.

She smiled, but still thought seriously and answered his somewhat strange question.

"If we have another life, that’s not something you need to worry about," she said. "In my next life, I’ll make you fall in love with me all over again."
"But what if I’m a bastard in my next life?" he asked.
"Have you ever heard of male and female evil spirits?" she teased, raising her eyebrows playfully.

He couldn't help but laugh after hearing this.

He made any answer for her in the next life.

But... he still fell in love with her in the next life.

In fact, he had done it many times.

When ‘restarting’ and ‘reading files’ for the same life goal,

He may have been suffering from this long process of 'reading files', but during the time he spent with her, he never got tired of it.

He stretched out his hand and hugged her.

She just kept quiet and hugged him,

"...If in the next life, there is a little girl who looks like you, has the same personality as you, and is also named as you, she says she likes me, but she is not you, what should I do?" He asked softly.
She whispered, "Then she is me."
